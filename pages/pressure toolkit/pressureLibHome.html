INTELLITEX

Fabric Input Library

Basic

Used to implement basic pressure sensing, based on material classification.

Composite Sponge Cloth

Material: top and bottom layers: polyester fabric, center layer: sponge

Thickness: 3mm or thicker

Feels like: upper and lower layers are normal fabric touch, with some pressing elasticity in the longitudinal direction

Interaction features:

  • capable of large area sensing
  • pressing: smaller fabric resistance; larger touch area: smaller resistance more significantly

Lambswool-like Fabric

Material: Polyester

Thickness: 4.5mm

Feels like: plush, soft

Interaction features:

  • soft, 3d,and plush interface
  • capable of large area sensing
  • pressing: smaller fabric resistance; larger touch area: smaller resistance more significantly

Spandex-Nylon Fabric

Material: 80%-90% Nylon, 20%-10% Spandex

Thickness: 0.6mm

Feels like: four-sided elastic with a cotton touch

Interaction features:

  • only capable of small area sensing (the distance between two silver fabric electrodes should not exceed 10mm)
  • pressing: smaller fabric resistance; larger touch area: smaller resistance more significantly

Wool Yarn Ball

Material: wool yarn

Radius: 20mm - 40mm

Feels like: feels like: soft, furry ball

Interaction features:

  • more vivid pressure sensing interface & interesting tactile feedback
  • the pressure sensing area is limited to the size of the wool ball
  • pressing: smaller fabric resistance

Advanced

Based on the principle of pressure sensing, realizing more different interaction effects.

Pressure Sensing x Switch

Material: spandex-nylon fabric, velcro, and base fabric of any material you like

Interaction features:

  • it can be used as a common fabric pressure sensor, and at the same time, it can play the role of switching on and off, rotating the sensing piece paste can make the pressure sensor disconnected from the circuit

Wool Yarn Ball Joystick

Material: wool yarn

Interaction features:

  • this three-dimensional fabric structure can be used as an input similar to a gamepad up/down/right/left/right stick
