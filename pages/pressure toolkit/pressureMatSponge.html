Pressure Sensor - Composite Sponge Cloth

What does the sensor do?

Used as a ordinary pressure sensing material, when pressure is applied to it, the resistance decreases. If the area over which the pressure is applied is larger, the resistance decreases more. Meanwhile, it can provide three-dimensional tactile feedback, depending on the specific thickness of the composite foam material.

You will need:

  • carbon black ink
  • composite sponge cloth
  • conductive silver fabric
